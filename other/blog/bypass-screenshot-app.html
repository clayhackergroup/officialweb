Bypass Screenshot Restriction (FLAG_SECURE) — Deep Guide for 2025

A complete explanation of Android’s FLAG_SECURE, how Sauce Labs & BrowserStack handle screenshot restrictions, apps that block screenshots, and legitimate ways QA teams can test protected screens.

Note: This guide is written for legitimate QA, engineering, and enterprise security teams. FLAG_SECURE is a security feature and must not be bypassed for malicious purposes.

What is FLAG_SECURE?

FLAG_SECURE is an Android capability that prevents screenshots, screen recording, mirroring, and streaming. Apps use it to protect sensitive information such as banking data, OTP screens, DRM-protected video, and private messages.

Why Screenshot Bypass is Needed During Testing?

Platforms like Sauce Labs, BrowserStack, and internal debug tools sometimes need to capture screens for QA, automated testing, and verifying UI flow. When FLAG_SECURE is active, these platforms show:

  • Black screen during live session
  • No video recorded for that section
  • Blank screenshots in automation

When Bypass Screenshot Restriction Works

Certain testing platforms allow bypass **only if**:

  • You own the source code
  • You disable FLAG_SECURE in that build
  • The APK is uploaded to your secure app storage
  • It's NOT a third-party app like Google Pay, Netflix, etc.

100% LEGAL Ways to Bypass It for Testing

  • Use a debug build with FLAG_SECURE disabled
  • Use BuildConfig.DEBUG flags to conditionally disable screenshot protection
  • Use enterprise test platforms (Sauce Labs / BrowserStack) that support controlled override
  • Use device-owner mode on enterprise devices (MDM / EMM)
  • Enable “Bypass Screenshot Restriction” on Sauce Labs for your own app

Why Third-Party Apps Cannot Be Bypassed

Apps like Google Pay, Netflix, and OTT platforms use highly strict FLAG_SECURE + DRM. They cannot be bypassed on any test platform unless the developer themselves disables the protection.

Final Note: Always test only your own apps. FLAG_SECURE is a security mechanism, not a restriction to be avoided.
