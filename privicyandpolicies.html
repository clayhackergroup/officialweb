NotifySoul Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Notify Soul ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Notify Soul collects and stores the following information:

  • Notification Data: Content of notifications from messaging apps (WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, etc.) including sender names, message text, and timestamps
  • Device Information: Android version, device model for app functionality
  • Ad-related Data: Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data for ad personalization

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Displaying notification history and deleted messages
  • Providing app features and functionality
  • Showing personalized advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Improving app performance and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

All notification data is stored LOCALLY on your device only.

  • We do NOT upload or transmit your notification data to any servers
  • Data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can clear your history at any time using the "Clear History" button

5. Third-Party Services

Notify Soul uses Google AdMob for advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics

Please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Notification Listener Permission

  • Read incoming notifications from messaging apps
  • Detect when notifications are deleted
  • Store notification content locally

This permission is ONLY used for app functionality. We do NOT share or sell your data.

7. Data Retention

  • Notification data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Premium feature unlock data is stored for 7 days after watching ads
  • You have full control to delete all data at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Notify Soul is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your Rights

  • Access your stored notification data
  • Delete all your data using "Clear History"
  • Revoke notification access permission at any time
  • Uninstall the app to permanently delete all data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app with an updated effective date.
